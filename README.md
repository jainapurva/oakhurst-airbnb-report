# Oakhurst Airbnb Investment Report

Interactive report analyzing 30 for-sale homes in Oakhurst, CA (near Yosemite) as short-term-rental (Airbnb) investments.

**Open `index.html` in any browser** (self-contained; photos stream from the Redfin CDN).

## Two scenarios (toggle at the top)
- **Conservative** — 45% occupancy, $450/night, guests capped at 10 → a flat ~$73,962/yr revenue for every home, so returns track purchase price. The cheap, close-in 3-beds win; 29 of 30 cash-flow positive.
- **bnbcalc default** — the platform's optimistic per-home estimates (up to 66% occ, 15–18 guests, $85K–$191K/yr).

## What's inside
- 30 homes (3+ bd, $300K–$800K, ≤ ~12 min to downtown Oakhurst).
- Per-home: projected revenue, cash-on-cash, cap rate, cash flow, DSCR, cash needed (bnbcalc expense/financing model).
- Top 15: photos, hot tub & HOA checks, STR-permit eligibility, per-home take, and links to Zillow / Redfin / live bnbcalc analysis.
- Yosemite Valley distance + drive-to-town.

Sources: Zillow (listings), bnbcalc (projections), Redfin (listing details), Madera County (STR ordinance). Not financial advice.
