Two scenarios. Conservative fixes occupancy at 45%, nightly rate at $450 and caps guests at 10 (3-bed≈8) — so projected revenue is a flat $73,962/yr for every home (45% × $450 × 365), and returns become driven almost entirely by purchase price. This deliberately strips out bnbcalc's optimistic, guest-inflated revenue (which assumed up to 15–18 guests and 66% occupancy). bnbcalc default shows the platform's original per-home estimate. All downstream metrics (cap, cash flow, cash-on-cash, DSCR, cash needed) are computed with bnbcalc's own expense & financing model (20% down, ~5.6% mortgage + property tax, flat $23.3K operating expenses, furnishing $8.5–15K by size).

STR permit (verified): whole-home STRs allowed in all residential zones of unincorporated Madera County; needs STR/TOT registration, business license, 9% TOT, fire inspection (~$196). Only Griffin & Goldside (Goldside Estates) carry an HOA. Sources: Madera County STVR Ordinance, Sierra News. Confirm current rules with the Madera County Planning Division.
