✅ STR permit — verified. In unincorporated Madera County (incl. Oakhurst), whole-home short-term rentals are permitted in all residential zones and “have always been legal” — no special-use permit or neighbor approval needed (that's only for B&Bs). Every home here is single-family residential, so all are zoning-eligible. You just need: STR permit registration + business license + 9% TOT + a passing fire inspection (~$196). Only per-parcel catch: HOA/CC&Rs can add limits — and only the two Goldside Estates homes (#1 Griffin, #2 Goldside) carry an HOA, so confirm their CC&Rs allow STR.
⚠️ Reality check on the projections. bnbcalc rates almost every Oakhurst home A+ and its revenue estimates run optimistic — the comparable Airbnbs it pulled average ~$54–60K/yr, yet it projects $85K–$191K here. Treat cash-on-cash above ~50% (the cheap 3-beds at 95–100%) as best-case. The most defensible numbers are the larger 4–6 bedroom homes. See my per-home take on each card below.
